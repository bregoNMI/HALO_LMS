What is a Headshot Photo?

Your Headshot Photo will be used for facial recognition purposes. Using this image, you will be able to login to your account and we may also monitor you while you are taking your course. Please carefully read through the list below to ensure that you correctly upload your Headshot Photo. You can either use the 'Take a Photo' option to capture a new image with your camera or upload an existing photo from your device.

Headshot Photo Requirements

You should not be smiling in the photo.
The photo must have been taken within 3 months of creating an account, and the learner must present the same characteristics contained on the photo.
Any change in appearance (hair color, haircut, beard, etc.) after photo is taken will require another photograph.
